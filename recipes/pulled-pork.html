Pulled Pork Recipe

How to make pulled pork, probably.

Ingredients Required

  • Boneless pork butt
  • brown sugar
  • kosher salt
  • smoked paprika, garlic powder, ground cumin, onion powder, freshly ground black pepper
  • vegetable oil
  • 12 oz of sweet sweet lager

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300*F and cut excess fat off your pig butt
  2. in a small bowl combine spices, salt, brown sugar and rub all over your pork butt ;)
  3. In a dutch oven heat the oil, and cook pork until browned on all sides
  4. Now pour beer around the pork and cover with the lid
  5. Bake for at least 3 hours, test tenderness of pork. Bake uncovered another 1-2 hours
  6. Test tenderness again, should come apart easily. If not bake longer
