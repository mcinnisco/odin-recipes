Cheesecake Recipe

How to make a cheesecake, probably.

Ingredients Required

  • Cream Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Sour cream
  • Strawberries (super optional)
  • Cornstarch

Steps

  1. Combine graham cracker crust and sugar and butter in a big ass bowl
  2. Press the mixture in to a greased cake pain (so it's crust shaped - make sure the crusts are high)
  3. Beat the cream cheese and sugar in to a beautiful filling, then stir in the eggs and a little bit of vanilla
  4. add eggs one at a time, mixing thoroughly
  5. Pour it in to your cake pan
  6. bake at 325*F for 45-50minutes, until the edges are golden but the middle still has a jiggle ;)
  7. Make strawberry topping if you want. I know there's strawberry topping in the photo, but I don't like strawberry. Plain all the way.
